Who We Are


Ursaworks is a student-run organization at Washington University in St. Louis that designs, builds, and competes with robots in the Robomaster competition. We are a diverse group of students from all different majors and backgrounds, and we are always looking for new members to join our team. We are a team of passionate students who are dedicated to learning and improving our skills in engineering, business, and leadership. We are always looking for new members to join our team, so if you are interested in joining, please contact us!
